Weak ▼

VWAP deviation strategy
Priority 1 · Biggest gap in existing code
Must add ▼
VWAP line±0.5% deviation entryVolume weightedIntraday
VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price) is the single most-watched level by professional crypto traders. Price far below VWAP = oversold relative to day's volume = buy. Price far above = overbought = sell. Already calculated in professional_bot.py but never used as a strategy signal — we just wire it up.
Regime: works best in RANGING and LOW_VOL. Estimated build time: 5 days with my help.
Bollinger Bands squeeze + breakout
Priority 2 · Improves regime detection
Must add ▼
BB width indicatorSqueeze detectionBreakout entryHIGH_VOL transition
Bollinger Bands narrow (squeeze) before big price moves. When bands are tighter than their 20-bar average, the market is coiling for a breakout. Enter when price finally breaks outside the bands with volume. This catches transitions from LOW_VOL to HIGH_VOL regime — exactly what the existing regime detector misses.
Regime: LOW_VOL transitioning to HIGH_VOL. Build time: 4 days.
MACD histogram divergence
Priority 3 · Better trend entries
High value ▼
MACD(12,26,9)Histogram directionSignal line cross
MACD measures momentum and trend together. When the MACD line crosses above its signal line AND the histogram is rising = strong buy. The histogram turning positive before a crossover gives earlier entry than EMA crossover alone. Works in TRENDING regime alongside the existing trend-following strategy.
Regime: TRENDING. Build time: 4 days.
Order book imbalance strategy
Priority 4 · Uses existing L2 data
Must add ▼
L2 orderbookBid/ask ratioTop 5 levelsPre-move signal
The bot already calls get_orderbook() for smart routing but throws away the data after. We use it: if total bid volume in top 5 levels is 70%+ of total, large buyers are stacked = price likely to rise soon. This signal fires BEFORE price moves — unlike every other strategy which reacts after. Unique edge.
Regime: all regimes. Build time: 6 days (most complex new addition).
Multi-timeframe confluence
Priority 5 · Cuts false signals in half
High value ▼
1m + 15m + 1hAll must agreeDirection filter
Current strategies only look at 1-minute candles. Problem: a 1m RSI signal against a strong 1h downtrend will lose almost every time. Fix: fetch 15m and 1h candles as well. Only enter a buy trade if the 1m, 15m AND 1h signals all agree direction is up. Fewer trades, much higher quality.
Works as a filter on top of any existing strategy. Build time: 6 days.
Funding rate extremes strategy
Priority 6 · Crypto-specific edge
Unique edge ▼
Delta API funding data8-hour cycleContrarian signal
Delta Exchange exposes funding rates via API. When funding is extremely positive (longs paying shorts a lot), the market is massively overleveraged long — a crash is likely. When extremely negative, shorts are crowded — a squeeze is coming. This is a contrarian signal unique to perpetual futures that traditional strategies cannot detect.
Regime: works in HIGH_VOL when combined with mean reversion. Build time: 6 days.
Volume-weighted momentum
Priority 7 · Upgrade existing momentum
Replaces momentum ▼
10-bar momentumVolume multiplierReplaces 3-bar version
Directly replaces the weak 3-bar momentum strategy. Uses 10-bar momentum multiplied by a volume ratio: if today's volume is 2× average, the momentum signal gets 2× confidence. A price move on high volume is real. A price move on thin volume is a trap. Simple but powerful upgrade.
Build time: 4 days. Replaces existing weak strategy.
Support / resistance breakout
Priority 8 · Classic crypto setup
Good addition ▼
20-bar high/lowVolume spikeBreakout confirmation
Identifies the highest high and lowest low over the last 20 bars as key levels. When price breaks above the 20-bar high with a volume spike (volume > 1.5× average), it's a confirmed breakout — buy. Below the 20-bar low with volume — sell. One of the most reliable patterns in crypto markets.
Regime: TRENDING and HIGH_VOL transitions. Build time: 5 days.
